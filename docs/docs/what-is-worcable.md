# What is Worcable?

Recruitment is one of the most important processes in any organization.

Yet in many companies it is still managed with:

- scattered emails
- spreadsheets that quickly become difficult to maintain
- expensive closed tools
- fragmented and unclear workflows

These methods slow down decision-making, scatter information, and make collaboration difficult.

**Worcable was born from a simple idea: recruitment deserves better tools.**

## A modern platform for recruitment

Worcable is an open source recruitment management platform (ATS — Applicant Tracking System) designed to help teams structure and manage their hiring process.

It provides everything needed to manage recruitment in one place:

- job postings
- candidate management
- recruitment pipelines
- interview feedback
- collaboration between recruiters and managers
- recruitment analytics

Instead of spreading information across multiple tools, Worcable centralizes the entire process into a single platform.

This gives teams a **clear and structured view of their hiring process**.

---

## Open source at its core

Worcable is **fully open source**.

This means anyone can:

- explore the code
- understand how the platform works
- adapt it to their organization
- contribute improvements

Recruitment is a universal challenge.  
The tools used to manage it should be open as well.

Open source enables:

- transparency
- control
- collaboration
- community-driven innovation

Worcable is not a closed product.

It is a project that anyone can help build.

---

## An alternative to proprietary ATS platforms

Many recruitment tools today are:

- expensive
- difficult to customize
- dependent on a vendor
- closed to community contributions

Worcable offers a different approach.

Organizations can:

- self-host the platform
- customize it for their workflows
- integrate it with existing tools
- keep full control of their data

---

## Built for flexibility

Every company hires differently.

Some rely heavily on technical interviews.  
Others focus on collaborative evaluation or structured hiring workflows.

Worcable was designed to be:

- modular
- flexible
- extensible

Organizations can adapt the platform to their hiring processes instead of adapting their processes to the tool.

---

## Accessible to every organization

Worcable aims to be useful for a wide range of teams:

- startups
- growing companies
- SMEs
- NGOs
- distributed teams

The goal is simple:

**make modern recruitment tools accessible to everyone.**

---

## A platform shaped by its community

Worcable is not a static product.

It evolves thanks to:

- feedback from users
- contributions from developers
- real-world hiring needs

Every contribution helps improve the platform and build a more open and effective recruitment tool.

---

## In summary

Worcable is:

- a recruitment management platform
- an open source project
- an alternative to proprietary ATS tools
- a community-driven initiative

Hiring great people is critical for every organization.

Worcable exists to make that process **simpler, more transparent, and more collaborative.**

**Worcable existe pour construire ces outils.**
