# Why Worcable?

Hiring the right people is one of the most important challenges organizations face.

Every hiring decision impacts a company's culture, performance, and long-term success.

Yet the tools used to manage recruitment are often:

- complex
- expensive
- closed
- difficult to adapt

Worcable was created to offer a different approach.

## Simplifying the hiring process

Recruitment involves many steps:

- publishing job offers
- receiving applications
- reviewing candidates
- conducting interviews
- collecting feedback
- making hiring decisions

Without the right tools, information quickly becomes scattered across emails, documents, and internal conversations.

Worcable centralizes the entire process into one platform so teams can work more efficiently.

This makes hiring:

- clearer
- more structured
- more collaborative

Recruiters and managers can easily track candidates and move faster when making decisions.

---

## Built on open source principles

Most Applicant Tracking Systems are proprietary.

This usually means:

- limited customization
- expensive subscriptions
- vendor lock-in
- little transparency

Worcable follows a different philosophy.

The platform is **open source**.

This allows organizations to:

- understand how the software works
- adapt the platform to their needs
- contribute improvements
- maintain full control over their data

Open source also encourages innovation through community collaboration.

---

## Designed for flexibility

No two companies hire in exactly the same way.

Some organizations rely on:

- multiple interview stages
- technical assessments
- collaborative evaluations
- managerial approvals

Worcable was designed to support these different workflows.

The platform provides structure while remaining flexible enough to adapt to each organization's hiring practices.

---

## Accessible to teams everywhere

Modern recruitment platforms are often built primarily for large enterprises.

Worcable aims to support a much wider range of organizations:

- startups
- small and medium businesses
- NGOs
- growing teams
- distributed companies

The goal is to make modern hiring tools **open, accessible, and adaptable**.

---

## Built with and for the community

Worcable is a living project.

Its evolution is driven by:

- feedback from users
- contributions from developers
- real hiring challenges faced by teams

Every contribution helps build a better and more open recruitment platform.

---

## In summary

Worcable exists to:

- simplify recruitment workflows
- structure hiring processes
- offer an open source alternative to proprietary ATS tools
- give organizations control over their recruitment infrastructure

Recruitment deserves better tools.

Worcable aims to build those tools **in an open and collaborative way.**
